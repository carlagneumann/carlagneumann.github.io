# ![](/uploads/tomato-bisque.jpg){: width="750" height="1334"}Tomato Bisque

Follow the steps:

1. Pre-heat oven 450 F;
2. Peal the tomatoes;
3. Bake them for 20 min;
4. Smash them in a pan;
5. Add salt & the butter.
